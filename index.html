About Me

Senyang Huang is an Assistant Professor at the University of the Faroe Islands. Senyang Huang is an independent researcher holding a doctoral degree in cryptography. His work focuses on foundational scientific problems in symmetric‑key cryptanalysis, where he has spent several years conducting deep and dedicated research. He has published research contributions at top international cryptography conferences.

Senyang maintains an open and collaborative research philosophy. He values interdisciplinary cooperation and strongly believes that combining perspectives from different fields can lead to breakthroughs that would not emerge otherwise.

His current research interests include:

  • Symmetric‑key cryptanalysis, especially SHA‑3 and related primitives
  • Secure implementation of cryptographic primitives
  • Security problems in machine‑learning models, including cryptanalytic extraction attacks
